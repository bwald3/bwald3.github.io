Description

Enhancing contact list and search filters for a simplier user experience.

Overview

The existing contact list was outdated and difficult to navigate, making searches inefficient. Users couldn’t save searches, leading to frustration and repetitive work.

I led the design direction and front-end development, focusing on improving usability, search functionality, and overall user experience.

The new platform introduced a modern interface, intuitive navigation, and enhanced search capabilities. Users could now filter results easily and save searches for future use, streamlining their workflow.
