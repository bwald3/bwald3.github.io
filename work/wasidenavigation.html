Overview

Users of our platform were struggling to navigate through our system quickly and effortlessly.

I led the design direction and overall user experience, along with the frontend development of a new navigation system.

The launch of the new navigation exceeded our expectations. Our community responded positively to the changes and expressed relief at the improved ease of navigation.

02

Problem

Users struggle to navigate the system quickly and effortlessly.

OLD
03

Research

Major Pain Points:

Lack of visual representation of each of the different navigation menu items

Having to scroll all the way to the top of a page to access navigation.

Difficult to operate on mobile devices.

04

Highlights

Fixed Menu

Use of a fixed side navigation so users can quickly navigate the system with unnecessary scrolling back upwards.

Visual Representation of Nav Items

Added Icons as the main focal point to enhance visual memory for users to easily navigate the system

Enhanced Mobile Menu

Simple Slideout menu that is easy to operate with touching on mobile

05

Conclusion

The redesign of the user navigation system proved to be a successful and highly impactful solution. By addressing the core pain points identified during research, the new navigation system turned out to not only be functional but intuitive and efficient. The addition of a fixed side menu, paired with easily recognizable icons, has significantly reduced user frustration and increased their ability to navigate the platform.

The positive feedback from the community confirms that the changes we made were well-received, demonstrating the importance of considering user feedback and usability in design. This project has set a new standard at Wise Agent for user experience on our platform and the importance of mobile design.
