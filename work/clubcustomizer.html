Overview

For the Orange Whip Club Customizer, I utilized WooCommerce to streamline the integration of the customization feature while focusing heavily on the front-end design. The primary goal was to update the style and ensure a visually engaging, user-friendly interface that allowed customers to personalize their products with a live preview.

With the backend already built out, I concentrated on improving the user experience by enhancing the styles, interactions, and overall visual presentation. The customizer provides a smooth, intuitive process for users to adjust color options, creating a personalized product before purchase. This approach ensured a scalable foundation for future features while keeping the user experience simple and effective.
