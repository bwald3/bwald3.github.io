Overview

I designed and developed a brand-new hunting land rental platform, making it simple for landowners to list their properties and for hunters to find available land. The goal was to create an intuitive and user-friendly experience that streamlined the rental process while maintaining a visually engaging and functional design.

I led the design direction, user experience strategy, and full development of the site, ensuring seamless navigation, clear call-to-action elements, and a responsive interface. Built entirely in WordPress, the platform balances performance, usability, and scalability, providing an efficient solution for both landowners and hunters.
