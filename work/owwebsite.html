Overview

Leading the homepage redesign for a website that needed a more modern, engaging, and user-friendly experience. The goal was to update the visual style, improve usability, and enhance content hierarchy to better align with current design trends and user expectations.

I focused on refining the layout, typography, and interactions to create a cleaner and more intuitive browsing experience. The new design emphasizes accessibility, responsiveness, and a visually compelling aesthetic while ensuring smooth navigation and clear calls to action sections.

Highlights

Enhanced Navigation

A new navigation and menu for updated look and to achieve simple navigation with quick access to top products.

New Product Slider

An engaging slider to drive traffic for the newest products released.

New grid system

A new grid system for the home page that showcases expected grid behavior and pushing the boundaries of the grid.

CTA Sections for product categories

Addition of simple CTA sections for product categories to drive traffic onto the shop pages.
