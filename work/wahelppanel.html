Help Panel

My Role

UI/UX Design | Frontend Development

Team

Derek Dorrance - Software Dev

Timeline

2 Months - Launched in August 2024

Overview

Users were skipping available resources and reaching out for help. This was due to inability and extra steps it took to find our knowledge base and videos.

I led the design and front-end development, focusing on simplifying access to help and enhancing usability.

Built out an in-app help panel, providing quick access to the knowledge base, related videos, system updates, and support contact — all within the app.

02

Problem

Users were skipping available resources and reaching out for help. This was due to inability and extra steps it took to find our knowledge base and videos.

03

Research

Major Pain Points:

Extra steps to find the help resources

Having to navigate multiple tabs back and forth

Ideation:

Quick panel with access to all of our help features

Access it directly from the page they are working on to avoid having to bounce around tabs

04

Highlights

Quickly access to helpful tools

Place to search our knowledge base and see related videos to the page they are on to reduce the need for having to contact support or look around for answers.

Easy way to contact support for help

A place for users to quickly reach out to support from inside the system so they don’t have to go through extra steps to get in touch with our team.

Feature updates section

View new features and important updates so users can see what’s new and get updates on the system.

Work and get help at the same time

With the panel and slideout combination, you can work directly on the page and search through the help resources when needed.

05

Conclusion

The introduction of the in-app Help Panel greatly simplified access to support resources, reducing unnecessary steps for users. With quick access to the knowledge base, related videos, feature updates, and an easy way to contact support, users are now able to find solutions more efficiently. This has led to fewer support inquiries and a more streamlined user experience. The Help Panel has proven to be a valuable addition, enhancing the platform’s usability.
